Save your About-section portrait here as:

    portrait.jpg   (or .webp / .png — then update profile.photo in
                    src/app/data/portfolio.ts to match the extension)

Recommended:
  - Roughly square (the frame uses a 1:1 aspect ratio), e.g. 800×800 or larger
  - Export as WebP or JPG and keep it under ~300 KB for fast loading

Until a file named portrait.jpg exists here, the About section shows a
styled "AS" placeholder instead of a broken image.
